Chocolate

Chocolate is a food made from roasted and ground cocoa beans that can be a liquid, solid, or paste, either by itself or to flavor other foods.

The shell is removed to reveal nibs, which are ground to chocolate liquor: unadulterated chocolate in rough form.

They are usually fermented to develop the flavor, then dried, cleaned, and roasted.

Cocoa beans are the processed seeds of the cacao tree (Theobroma cacao).
